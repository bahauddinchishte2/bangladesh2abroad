# DAAD Scholarship Germany 2024

The German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD) offers fully-funded scholarships for international students to pursue master's degrees at German universities. This prestigious scholarship program aims to support academic exchange and provide opportunities for future leaders.

### Additional Benefits
- Pre-departure language preparation
- Orientation course upon arrival
- Integration into German academic culture
- Network of international scholars
- Career development support

## Eligibility Requirements

### Academic Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree with excellent grades
- Not more than 6 years since last degree
- Minimum CGPA of 3.0 (on a 4.0 scale)
- Relevant academic background for chosen program

### Language Requirements
- German: B2 level (for German-taught programs)
- English: IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL iBT 90 (for English-taught programs)

### Additional Criteria
- Maximum age limit: 32 years
- At least two years of work experience (preferred)
- Clear research/study proposal
- Strong motivation letter

## Application Process

### Step 1: Program Selection
1. Choose study program and university
2. Verify admission requirements
3. Check language prerequisites
4. Prepare application documents

### Step 2: Required Documents
- Completed DAAD application form
- CV/Resume in German or English
- Letter of motivation (1-3 pages)
- Research proposal/study plan
- Academic certificates and transcripts
- Language certificates
- Two recommendation letters
- Passport copy
- Portfolio (if applicable)

### Step 3: Submission
1. Create DAAD portal account
2. Fill online application
3. Upload required documents
4. Submit before deadline
5. Send hard copies if required

## Selection Criteria
