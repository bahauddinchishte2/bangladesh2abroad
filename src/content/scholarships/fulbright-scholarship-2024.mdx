# Fulbright Scholarship 2024-25 for Bangladeshi Students

The Fulbright Foreign Student Program is the flagship international educational exchange program sponsored by the U.S. government. This prestigious scholarship offers opportunities for graduate students from Bangladesh to pursue Master's degrees in the United States.

## Scholarship Overview

The Fulbright Scholarship covers:
- Full tuition and fees
- Monthly living stipend
- Health insurance
- Round-trip airfare
- Book and settling-in allowance

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible, candidates must:
- Be Bangladeshi citizens
- Hold a minimum of a 4-year bachelor's degree
- Have at least 16 years of education
- Demonstrate English language proficiency (minimum IELTS score of 7.0)
- Have at least 2 years of work experience (preferred)
- Not have extensive experience in the U.S.

## Application Process

1. **Online Application**
   - Complete the online application form
   - Submit all required documents
   - Provide academic transcripts and certificates

2. **Required Documents**
   - Updated CV/Resume
   - Statement of Purpose
   - Three letters of recommendation
   - IELTS/TOEFL score report
   - Academic transcripts
   - Copy of passport

3. **Selection Process**
   - Initial screening
   - Written test
   - Interview
   - Final selection

## Important Dates

- Application Opens: January 15, 2024
- Application Deadline: March 31, 2024
- Written Test: May 2024
- Interviews: June 2024
- Final Results: July 2024
- Program Start: Fall 2025

For more information and to apply, visit the [official website](https://bd.usembassy.gov/education-culture/programs/fulbright-program/).
