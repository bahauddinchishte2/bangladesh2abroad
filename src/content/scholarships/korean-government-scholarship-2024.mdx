# Korean Government Scholarship Program (KGSP) 2024

The Korean Government Scholarship Program (KGSP) is a prestigious scholarship offered by the National Institute for International Education (NIIED) to international students who wish to pursue higher education in South Korea.

## Scholarship Benefits

- Full tuition fee coverage
- Monthly living allowance (1,000,000 KRW for master's students)
- Settlement allowance (200,000 KRW upon arrival)
- One-way air ticket (economy class)
- Korean language training (1 year)
- Medical insurance
- Korean proficiency grants
- Research support grants for degree students

## Eligibility Requirements

1. **Nationality**
   - Applicants and their parents must be citizens of countries other than Korea
   - Overseas Korean adoptees are eligible

2. **Age Limit**
   - Under 40 years of age as of September 1, 2024

3. **Academic Requirements**
   - Bachelor's degree completed or expected by August 31, 2024
   - Minimum GPA of 2.64 on a 4.0 scale, 2.80 on a 4.3 scale, 2.91 on a 4.5 scale, or 3.23 on a 5.0 scale

4. **Health**
   - Must be in good health, both physically and mentally

5. **Language Proficiency**
   - No minimum TOPIK score required at the time of application
   - English proficiency preferred but not mandatory

## Available Fields of Study

- Liberal Arts and Social Sciences
- Science, Engineering, and Technology
- Arts and Sports
- Medicine and Healthcare

## Application Process

1. **Choose Application Track**
   - University Track: Apply through a specific Korean university
   - Embassy Track: Apply through the Korean embassy in your country

2. **Required Documents**
   - Application form
   - Personal statement and study plan
   - Two letters of recommendation
   - Academic transcripts and certificates
   - Medical examination forms
   - Copy of passport
   - Certificates of language proficiency (if applicable)

3. **Selection Process**
   - Document screening
   - Interview (for shortlisted candidates)
   - Final selection by NIIED

## Important Dates

- Application Opens: February 1, 2024
- Embassy Track Deadline: March 31, 2024
- University Track Deadline: Varies by university
- Final Results: June 2024
- Korean Language Course: September 2024
- Degree Program Start: March 2025

## Additional Information

### Korean Language Program
- One-year intensive Korean language training
- Must achieve TOPIK Level 3 to start degree program
- Additional 6-month language training if TOPIK Level 3 not achieved

### University Placement
- Embassy track candidates can choose up to 3 universities
- Final placement decided by NIIED
- Over 60 Korean universities participating

### Research Program
- Access to state-of-the-art research facilities
- Opportunities for collaboration with Korean researchers
- Participation in international conferences
